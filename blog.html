Background of Finance

Finance is well known as financial management. The discipline may be viewed as an art and science of managing financial resources so as to achieve goals.

The discipline is broadly grouped into two: Public Finance; and Private Finance. We could further categorise Private Finance into: Business/Corporate Finance; and Personal Finance.


Personal Finance

Personal finance is the process or art of planning and managing personal financial activities. An individual may want to plan on thier income sources, spending habbits, savings culture, and investment. Managing one's personal finances can be summarized in a budget or financial plan.


Personal finance introduces the concept of financial independence. Financial independence is the status of having enough income (from investments, passive businesses, real estate, etc.) to pay for one's reasonable living expenses without having to rely on formal employment.

Most challenges faced by individual regarding financial independence has to with financial planning. Individuals for many reasons are not able to plan their finances which leads them to personal financial crunch.
